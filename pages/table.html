Testing Scenarios

Here are some test cases to automate:

  • Sort: Click the "Name" or "Salary" headers and verify the rows are sorted accordingly.
  • Search: Enter a value in the search box and verify only matching rows are displayed.
  • Pagination: Click "Next" and verify the table content changes.
  • Data Extraction: Extract all email addresses or salaries and calculate a total.
